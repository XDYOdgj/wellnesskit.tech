Introducing the FUNCCUBE Electric Spin Scrubber

Redevelop your cleaning experience with this modern, cordless tool designed for comfort and efficiency. Whether you're in the bathroom, kitchen, or anywhere else, the FUNCCUBE makes cleaning easier and more enjoyable, especially for those with mobility challenges.

Ergonomic & Cordless Design

Enjoy hands-free cleaning with the FUNCCUBE's cordless, ergonomic build. No more bending or kneeling, making it ideal for people with back pain or limited mobility. The lightweight frame allows for easy handling without fatigue.

Versatile Brush Options

Choose from 4 detachable brush lengths to tackle different surfaces like grout, tiles, or even hardwood floors. The adjustable brush makes the FUNCCUBE perfect for a variety of cleaning needs, ensuring optimal results every time.

Two Speed Settings

With 2 adjustable speed levels (250/350 RPM), the FUNCCUBE allows you to customize your cleaning style. Lighter settings are great for delicate surfaces, while higher power is ideal for tough stains and dirt.

IPX7 Waterproof & Durable

Designed for use in wet environments, the FUNCCUBE has an IPX7 waterproof rating. This means you can safely clean anywhere in the home, including showers and baths, without worrying about water damage or electrical issues.

Type-C Fast Charging

The FUNCCUBE features a Type-C fast charging port and a built-in battery level display, so you can keep track of your power and clean without interruptions. This makes it a reliable tool for all your cleaning needs.

Simple Assembly & Use

Quick and easy to assemble, the FUNCCUBE is designed for hassle-free use. It's an ideal choice for anyone looking for an efficient and user-friendly cleaning solution that fits into your lifestyle.
